RiverRiders Fernie

Our mission is to provide unforgettable rafting experiences in the heart of the Canadian Rockies. We strive to connect people with nature and create lasting memories.

History

RiverRiders Fernie was founded in 1995 by a group of local river enthusiasts. Over the years, we've grown to become the leading provider of rafting experiences in the area.

Adventure Awaits You!

Our Rafting Trips

Choose from a variety of exciting rafting trips on the Elk River, right here in beautiful Fernie, BC.

  • Half-Day Family Rafting

    Gentle rapids for all ages. Minimum age: 6 years. Difficulty: Easy.

  • Full-Day Whitewater Challenge

    Test your skills on thrilling rapids. Difficulty: Moderate to Advanced.

  • Multi-Day River Expedition

    Explore the wilderness with an extended trip. Difficulty: Varies.

Safety First

Your safety is our priority. We provide all necessary safety equipment and our guides are certified professionals.
