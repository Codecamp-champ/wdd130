About RiverRiders Fernie Rafting

Fernie RiverRiders has been providing thrilling rafting experiences on the Elk River for over 20 years. Founded by local adventurers, we've grown from a small operation to a leading rafting company in the Canadian Rockies. Our passion for the river and commitment to safety have made us a favorite among locals and tourists alike.
