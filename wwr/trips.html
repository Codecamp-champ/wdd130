Our Rafting Adventures

Explore Our Trips

Half-Day Scenic Float

Enjoy a relaxing float down a calmer section of the Elk River. Perfect for families and those looking for a gentle introduction to rafting. Includes snacks and refreshments.

  • Duration: 3-4 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Price: $75 per person

Full-Day Adventure Challenge

Experience the thrill of class III and IV rapids on this action-packed full-day trip. Includes a riverside lunch and all necessary safety gear.

  • Duration: 6-8 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging
  • Price: $140 per person

Multi-Day Wilderness Expedition

Immerse yourself in the stunning backcountry of the Elk River on a multi-day expedition. Includes camping gear, meals, and expert guides for an unforgettable wilderness experience.

  • Duration: 2-3 days
  • Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging
  • Price: $450 per person (minimum 4 people)
